# Productivity Prompts

Prompts for planning, focus, and turning ideas into action.

---

## Daily Builder Plan

```txt
Act as a practical productivity coach.

Help me plan a focused building session.

My goal: [insert goal]
Available time: [insert time]
Energy level: [low/medium/high]

Give me:
- one clear priority
- 3 small tasks
- what to avoid
- a realistic stopping point
```

---

## Idea Prioritizer

```txt
Act as a product-minded creative strategist.

Help me prioritize these ideas:

[insert ideas]

Rank them by:
- easiest to start
- highest learning value
- strongest portfolio value
- most aligned with my long-term direction
```
