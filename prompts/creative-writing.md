# Creative Writing Prompts

Prompts for lyrics, storytelling, ideas, and emotional writing.

---

## Lyric Mood Prompt

```txt
Act as a creative songwriting assistant.

Help me write lyric ideas based on this mood:

Mood: [insert mood]

Style: emotional, direct, poetic, modern

Give me:
- 3 possible lyric lines
- 1 hook idea
- 1 short concept for a full song
```

---

## Story Concept Prompt

```txt
Act as a creative writing partner.

Help me develop a story concept based on this theme:

Theme: [insert theme]

Give me:
- a short premise
- the main character
- the conflict
- the emotional core
- a possible ending
```
