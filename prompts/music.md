# Music Prompts

Prompts for lyrics, artist identity, song concepts, and creative direction.

---

## Song Concept Prompt

```txt
Act as a creative music strategist and songwriting partner.

Help me develop a song concept based on this idea:

Idea: [insert idea]

Give me:
- a song title
- the emotional theme
- the main story
- the target mood
- 3 possible lyric lines
- 1 hook idea
```

---

## Artist Identity Prompt

```txt
Act as a creative brand strategist for an artist.

Help me define an artist identity based on:

Name/alias: [insert name]
Style: [insert style]
Themes: [insert themes]

Give me:
- core identity
- visual direction
- tone of voice
- 3 content ideas
- 3 possible taglines
```

---

## Lyric Refinement Prompt

```txt
Act as a songwriting editor.

Improve these lyrics while keeping the original emotion and voice:

[insert lyrics]

Make them:
- more direct
- more poetic
- more memorable
- less generic

Give me:
- improved version
- 3 alternative lines
- short explanation of what changed
```
