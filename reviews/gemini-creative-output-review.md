# Gemini Creative Output Review Prompt

Use this prompt when reviewing generated lyrics, prompts, creative workflows, or product ideas.

---

## Prompt

```txt
You are acting as an external creative AI reviewer.

Project:
[project name]

Input:
[paste mood, theme, style, preset, or prompt]

Output:
[paste generated lyrics, prompt result, or creative output]

Evaluate the output for:
- creative usefulness
- emotional strength
- specificity
- originality
- cliché level
- clarity
- beginner friendliness
- practical usability

Give me:

1. Overall rating from 1-10

2. Strengths

3. Weaknesses

4. Lines or sections that feel generic

5. Suggestions to make it more specific and usable

6. Prompt improvements

7. Product ideas this output suggests
```

---

## Best used for

- Lyric output review
- Prompt quality review
- Creative workflow review
- AI-Prompt-Library content review
- App/product idea refinement
